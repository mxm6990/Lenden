# Lenden — Compliance Notes

> Prototype documentation only. Not legal advice.

## Regulatory posture

Lenden v1 is a **mock prototype**. It must not imply live securities services, BSEC licensing, or regulatory approval.

## Items requiring expert review before production

- **BSEC review** — Any live brokerage-facing feature requires appropriate securities regulation alignment in Bangladesh.
- **CDBL BO account process** — Beneficiary Owner account opening must align with a licensed Depository Participant partner.
- **KYC / AML** — Identity verification, AML screening, and ongoing monitoring must be reviewed with local compliance counsel.
- **Data protection & cybersecurity** — Storage and processing of NID, financial, and session data must comply with applicable Bangladeshi law, including expectations under the Cyber Security Act 2023.
- **Prototype language** — All user-facing copy must avoid claiming live trading, licensing, or financial advice.

## Safe prototype wording

Use phrases such as:

- Prototype environment
- Mock trading only
- Market data shown for demonstration only
- Not financial advice
- Securities services require licensed brokerage partnerships and regulatory approval

## Final note

Final compliance implementation requires review by Bangladeshi legal and regulatory experts.
